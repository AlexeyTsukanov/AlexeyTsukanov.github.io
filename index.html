Teaching and Coaching

We are active dance teachers. Working with couples all ages and categories Latin and Standard disciplines. Most of our couples are top dancers in Switzerland.

Dance school

Our location is dance school Dance Unlimited Zurich. We are giving privat lessons and groups for competitive dancers, kids and performance practice groups. more information on duz.ch

Shows

Choreographing and dancing shows. People are inviting us to make their birthday brigter, musicals more special and foreach order we choreograph special dance.

Modelin

Each product or promotion needs a good presentation. In our offer is to perform it as best as possible. We are assistin, taking arts in photo or video advertising or creating a dance show for your special topic
